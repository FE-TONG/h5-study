2、CSS3 单词自动换行

在CSS3中的属性word-wrap 允许英文单词文本强制进行换行，格式如下：

word-wrap:break-word(值); 词内换行

（1）对照组word-wrap: ;

Passion is sweet Love makes weak You said you cherised freedom soooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooo You refused to let it go Follow your faith

（2）实验组word-wrap:break-word;

Passion is sweet Love makes weak You said you cherised freedom soooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooo You refused to let it go Follow your faith

3、CSS3 自定义字体

在CSS3中的属性@font-face{font-family: name; src:url(1), url(2);} 可以自定义你要给网页使用的字体，格式如下：

@font-face{font-family: name; src:url(路径1), url(路径2);} -------自定义字体;

@font-face{font-family: dear5609; src:url(font/Sansation_Bold.eot), url(font/Sansation_Bold.ttf);}

(1)未调用 ;

Well I wonder could it be When I was dreaming about you baby You were dreaming of me Call me crazy Call me blind To still be suffering is stupid after all of this time

(2)调用：.txt6{font-family:"dear5609";}
